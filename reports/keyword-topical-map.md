## Content Expansion Rules

For each non-legal content page:
- Put the live calculator/tool above the fold.
- Use one H1 matching the page's primary keyword.
- Add an extractive 40-60 word answer near the top.
- Include formula, worked examples, common mistakes, and conversion tables where relevant.
- Add at least 5 FAQs that answer long-tail queries from the same cluster.
- Add internal links to parent hub, sibling tools, and next-step guide pages.
- Keep UI consistent: calculator first, short answer, table/reference section, guide content, related tools, FAQ.
- Avoid filler just to hit 2,000 words. The target should be met with examples, tables, troubleshooting, and workflow-specific guidance.
